About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), working at the intersection of Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and AI for healthcare and agriculture. My work focuses on lightweight and explainable deep models for medical image analysis and plant disease detection, bridging research with real-world impact.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science & Technology (2023), M.Eng. with Distinction in Information Systems in R&D Organizations (2019), and B.Eng. in Informatics & Computer Engineering (2017). I publish as first author in journals such as Applied Soft Computing, Knowledge-Based Systems, Biomedical Signal Processing and Control, IEEE Access, IET Image Processing, Life, Heliyon, and Neural Computing & Applications.

Beyond research, I enjoy mentoring students and collaborating across disciplines. I actively serve the community as a Reviewing Editor within Springer Nature’s Reviewer Communities.

Research Interests

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Developing intelligent systems and learning algorithms that mimic human reasoning for real-world applications, including healthcare, agriculture, and automation.

Internet of Things (IoT)

Integrating AI with IoT systems for smart environments — enabling data-driven decision-making in precision agriculture, health monitoring, and intelligent automation.

Large Multimodal Models (LMMs)

Exploring multimodal AI systems that process and reason across diverse inputs — text, images, and sensor data — to build robust, explainable, and human-centric solutions.

AI for Smart Precision

Designing intelligent models for precision agriculture, environmental monitoring, and sustainable smart city systems.

AI for Healthcare

AI-driven diagnostic tools, predictive modeling, and medical image analysis for early disease detection, prognosis, and treatment planning.

Generative AI

Harnessing the power of generative models (GANs, diffusion models, and transformers) to create innovative solutions in image synthesis, data augmentation, and creative AI applications.
