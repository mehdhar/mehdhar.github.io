About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), working at the intersection of Machine Learning and Computer Vision.

My research focuses on developing lightweight and explainable deep models tailored for resource-constrained environments, with impactful applications in medical image analysis (Mpox detection) and smart agriculture (Plant disease classification).

I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science & Technology (2023) and serve as a Reviewing Editor for Springer Nature. I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical AI models and practical, life-saving applications.
