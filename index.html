About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at UESTC, working at the intersection of Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and AI for healthcare and agriculture. My work focuses on lightweight and explainable deep models for medical image analysis and plant disease detection, bridging research with real-world impact.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science & Technology (2023), M.Eng. with Distinction in Information Systems in R&D Organizations (2019), and B.Eng. in Informatics & Computer Engineering (2017). I publish as first author in journals such as Applied Soft Computing, Knowledge-Based Systems, Biomedical Signal Processing and Control, IEEE Access, and Neural Computing & Applications.

Beyond research, I enjoy mentoring students and collaborating across disciplines. I actively serve the community as a Reviewing Editor within Springer Nature‚Äôs Reviewer Communities.
